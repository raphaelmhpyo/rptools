Basic ROTEM - Algorithm

1. PPH, Trauma, or ML > 5%

  • TXA indicated, or EXCESS FIBRINOLYSIS present
  • Give TXA 1g

2. FIBTEM A5 < 10mm

  • Indicates LOW FIBRINOGEN
  • Give Cryoprecipitate 10U
  • NB: 5 units of Cryoprecipitate increases FIBTEM A5 by ~2mm

3. EXTEM A5 < 35mm

  • Indicates LOW PLATELETS
  • Give Platelets 1 pool

4. EXTEM CT > 80s

  • Is FIBTEM A5 > 10mm?
    • Indicates LOW CLOTTING FACTORS with NORMAL FIBRINOGEN
    • Give FFP 2U
  • Is FIBTEM A5 < 10mm?
    • If EXTEM CT 80-140s:
      • Indicates LOW FIBRINOGEN ONLY (without low clotting factors necessarily)
      • Give Cryoprecipitate 10U and reassess
    • If EXTEM CT > 140s:
      • Indicates LOW CLOTTING FACTORS and LOW FIBRINOGEN
      • Give Cryoprecipitate 10U + FFP 2U

5. If HEPARIN is involved:

  • Inform ICU that HEPTEM is required
  • Compare INTEM CT vs HEPTEM CT
    • If INTEM CT > 205s and:
      • If HEPTEM CT < 205s:
        • The coagulopathy corrects with HEPARINASE (ie. removal of heparin)
        • Indicates HEPARIN EFFECT
        • Give Protamine
      • If HEPTEM CT > 205s:
        • Possible PROTAMINE EXCESS
        • Seek HAEMATOLOGY input
